  2. Audio

Audio tag

used to add audio to the webpage

Attributes of audio tag

  1. source : src(absolute or relative)
  2. controls : displays the control buttons like (mute,play,pause,speed)
  3. autoplay : to automatically play the song
  4. muted : it is used to mute the audio
  5. loop : it repeats the audio after completion of its duration

Video Tag

Displays the video in web page

Attributes of Video tag

  1. src : source(absolute and relative path)
  2. controls
  3. loop
  4. autoplay
  5. muted
  6. width
  7. height
  8. poster:it is like a thumbnail
